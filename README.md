# Email Front-End Portfolio

This repository contains sample **HTML email templates** demonstrating my skills in front-end development, with a focus on **responsive, cross-client email coding**.

These projects showcase my ability to convert designs into **pixel-perfect, table-based HTML emails** suitable for major email clients (Gmail, Outlook, Apple Mail) and ESP platforms (Mailchimp, HubSpot, Salesforce Marketing Cloud).

---

## **Samples**

### 1. Classic Email
- Simple, clean, and responsive design.
- Table-based layout with inline CSS.
- CTA button included.


### 2. Image & CTA Email
- Includes header image/banner and call-to-action button.
- Fully responsive and mobile-friendly.
- Perfect for marketing campaigns.


### 3. Newsletter / Multi-Section Email
- Multi-section layout with images and text.
- Mobile-responsive with stackable sections.
- Suitable for monthly newsletters and updates.

## **Technologies Used**
- HTML5
- CSS3 (inline)
- Responsive email design
- Table-based layouts for cross-client compatibility
- ESP platforms: Mailchimp, HubSpot, Salesforce Marketing Cloud
- Testing Tools: Litmus, Email on Acid
